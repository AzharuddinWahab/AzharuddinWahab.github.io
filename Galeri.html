Warisan - Sudirman

Warisan by Allahyarham Sudirman Haji Arshad

Created by Allahyarham Syed Haron Syed Ahmad.


The song 'Warisan' is a song that was popularized by Sudirman Haji Arshad. He is a figure who has been highly respected by the people of Malaysia to this day. He won the Bintang RTM competition in 1976. His excellence in the field of singing earned him titles such as "People's Entertainer," "People's Artist," and "Elvis Malaysia." His greatness in the field of singing is evident, and he is still remembered to this day.Born in Temerloh, Pahang, he was not only an entertainer but also engaged in side roles such as lyricist, author of books, and painter. Most of his works have provided valuable lessons and moral values to the local community, including the song 'Warisan'

This song was written by Syed Haron in 1980. However, it was only sung by Sudirman in 1982. This was because the songwriter had not yet found a suitable candidate to perform the song. Every lyric conveyed by the writer and the singer is very profound and should be carefully observed by all parties. Because it has its own values and implied meanings in the song, this song was chosen as the theme for Malaysia's 57th Independence Day. According to the Minister of Communication and Multimedia at the time, Datuk Seri Ahmad Shabery Cheek, the selection of this song as the theme in 2014 was considered very special and touched the hearts deeply.
